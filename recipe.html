Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Make a dough with the ingredients.
  2. Shape into balls and deep fry.
  3. Soak in sugar syrup.
  4. Serve warm.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Boil the milk and reduce it.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom.
  3. Set in a tray and cut into pieces.
  4. Serve chilled.

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Make chenna (cottage cheese) from milk.
  2. Drain excess water and knead until smooth.
  3. Shape into small balls and cook in sugar syrup.
  4. Serve chilled.

Mysore Park

Ingredients

  • Gram flour
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan.
  2. Add gram flour and roast until golden.
  3. Add cardamom and mix well.
  4. Set in a tray and cut into pieces.
